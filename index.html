About

David H. Bown, Musician, Teacher, Educator & Drummer. David has been on the Inland Empire music scene since 1988. David has been the driving back beat to famous groups such as Hindsight 20/20, Cry Havoc, Batallion & many others. David is also co-owner of Music Lessons Central located in Redlands, California where he teaches students of any age to be the best drummer they can be.
